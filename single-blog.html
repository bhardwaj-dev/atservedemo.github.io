MSG91 for Startups !!

Messaging Giant !! MSG91 was the first choice and the most suitable option we found for our OTP and Transactional updates for Our Startup. We started building AtServe, a Saas Platform for local suppliers and small vendors selling door to door, daily needs such as Mineral Water, Milk, Vegetables. While we were developing our signup, we came up with different ideas of signing up with social medias SDKs, but we couldn’t oversee the fact that we needed mobile number verification as the sole data to rely on our delivery based product, where each supplier has to contact their customers. This was the time when we came across our rescuer MSG91.

MSG91’s provides its segmented features of OTP based, Transactional based and Promotional based Mobile SMS options. We chose OTP and it’s really working fine for us, we haven’t got a single complaint where our users have to say, “I didn’t get the OTP”. Even the transactional SMS are hope in the darkness of Offline, as sometimes the internet is not available, our detailed SMS of new orders makes sure that the Vendor is updated with it.

MSG91’s approach to standing in contribution is appreciable.Since we are in operations from past 1 month, we have experienced how well it is working for us.

Our Technical Team, they could integrate it without any difficulty, and personal accounts at MSG91 gives you enough band of SMS where you can design, build, and test your product. If you are working on your new thrilling Idea of Startup and building an app, and need SMSs, Just go for MSG91 without even a second thought.It’s the best reliable messaging platform we would recommend to anyone.

Don’t worry if you don’t want to spend money at initial days, still validating through your products and features, just write to MSG91 team, stating your Startup details, your requirements, they have a swift response time, as per I had experience.
As a Founder and CEO of my Startup, I would recommend MSG91 Platform for starting your venture.
